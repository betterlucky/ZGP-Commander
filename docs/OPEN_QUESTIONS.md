# Open Questions

Status: **non-blocking; resolve through prototypes and playtesting**

Last updated: 2026-07-13

These questions should be answered through later slices and playtesting rather
than delaying the current playable loop. They are recorded to prevent
provisional guesses becoming permanent rules.

## Balance

- At what roster size do operational coverage and replacement depth normally
  become sufficient? Roughly 30 is a discussion reference, not a cap.
- How quickly should support-capacity expansion costs rise?
- How many routine, faction and urgent offers produce useful choice without
  making the board feel like a checklist?
- At what deployed squad size do ammunition and lost opportunity normally
  outweigh additional security?
- How strongly do movement, stationary reloads, scavenging and first aid reduce
  defensive output?
- What combination of injury effects produces deterioration without creating an
  unrecoverable campaign spiral?

## Campaign content

- Which factions and proper names survive the lore reduction?
- Which base facilities deserve distinct specialist assignments?
- What are the useful sources and sinks for ammunition, medical supplies and
  construction materials?
- How do transferred people become eligible for NPC appearances or return offers?
- Which distress scenarios contain a fighting allied squad versus incapacitated
  or captive subjects?
- Does endless play eventually include an optional nonlethal asynchronous
  challenge mode, and if so is it squad-, scenario- or defence-focused?

## Presentation

- How much solid geometry and ambient animation does the living base schematic
  need before it feels inhabited?
- Which survivor details require portraits versus generated dossier graphics?
- How are weapon, equipment and injury telemetry encoded clearly at normal
  tactical zoom?
- What audio vocabulary best maintains human attachment through an abstract feed?

## Technical validation

- What is the performance budget for fully simulated contacts, separate from the
  current 100-contact rendering benchmark?
- Which deterministic guarantees are necessary for replays and seeded scenario
  tests?
- When does the prototype source layout need to be split into the production
  module structure described in `ARCHITECTURE.md`?
